About Taiwan

Taiwan is a beautiful island to visit. I have lived here for the past couple months and have been able to explore almost all the major destinations. My favorite images are photographed in Tainan, Tapei, Hualien, and Kaohsiung. The photo gallery displays my favorite sights and top must places in each of the major cities. I have shared my favorite places to stay in each of these cities if you are interested in visiting!
