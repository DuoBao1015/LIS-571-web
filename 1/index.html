Inspiration Through Awe-Walking

An “awe walk” is a stroll in which you intentionally shift your attention outward instead of inward.


In Fall 2020, Neuroscientist Dr. Virginia Sturm lead a scientific study that
showed that going on 15 minute walks looking for things that inspire awe
can promote brain health in older adults.


This site was created to promote awe walking as a wellness practice by encouraging walkers of all ages to
use thematic prompts and creative modalities to explore their surroundings and share awe finds with one another.
We invite you to explore the map and gallery and to contribute with your own awe finds! Remember awe inspires awe so the
more you tune into this emotion the more awe inspiring things you will find!


How can creative prompts help us tune into moments of awe around us?
How can creative modes of documentation help us share those moments with others?



This website seeks to encourage this wellness practice by providing an interactive
space to share our creative encounters in awe. We welcome awe walkers of all ages.
