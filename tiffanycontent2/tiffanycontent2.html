Where is Kota Kinabalu?

Sabah is the second largest state in Malaysia and Kota Kinabalu is the capital of Sabah. Kota Kinabalu is famous for breathtaking views like their crystal clear water and abundant rainforests. There is so much to explore here. I will share my favorite pictures from the trip and I hope you can visit one day!
